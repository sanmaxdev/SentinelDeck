# Code of Conduct

## Our pledge

We want SentinelDeck to be a welcoming, harassment-free project for everyone,
regardless of experience level or background.

## Expected behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive in issues, pull requests, and discussions.
- Assume good intent and offer help rather than judgement.
- Keep the project focused on defensive, authorised security work.

## Unacceptable behaviour

- Harassment, personal attacks, or discriminatory language.
- Sharing techniques intended for unauthorised or malicious use.
- Sustained disruption of discussions or review.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or reject contributions and comments that violate
this code, and may block repeat offenders. To report a concern, contact the
maintainers privately through the repository's Security tab or a direct message.

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the spirit of the Contributor Covenant.
